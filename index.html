OptiBulk Pro: The Ultimate Privacy-First Media Suite

OptiBulk Pro is a professional-grade image compressor and PDF management tool that works 100% offline. No files ever leave your device.

Key Features:

  • Exact KB Compressor: Hit specific file size targets like 100KB for government portals and social media.
  • Batch Format Converter: Convert HEIC, WebP, AVIF, PNG, and JPG locally.
  • Secure PDF Suite: Merge, split, and reorder PDF pages without server uploads.
  • Privacy Scrub: Remove GPS and camera metadata from your photos instantly.

Why Choose Local Processing?

Most online tools upload your data to a server, creating privacy risks and storage limits. OptiBulk Pro uses your browser's local processing power (WASM) to give you unlimited, secure, and instant results even without an internet connection.

Frequently Asked Questions

How can I compress a photo to exactly 100KB?

Use our 'Exact Size Lock' feature. Select your files, enter '100' in the KB field, and our engine will perform iterative loops to find the best quality that stays under the limit.

Are my images safe?

Yes. We don't have a backend. We don't have a database. Your data is processed in RAM and destroyed when you close the tab.
